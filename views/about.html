Information

  • Who are you and why are you doing this ?
    We are group of IT professionals who have closely worked with health informatics systems and want to make the world a better place through our skills. As the entire world is sufferring we find it our responsibility to put our knowledge to a good use by providing relevant and easy to understand information on COVID19. to the citizens.
  • Is this an official dashboard ?
    No, this is a volunteer driven initiative. We do not represent any government or government organization.
  • What is the source of data ?
    We source our data from Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India.
  • Why do you use data only from Ministry of Health and Family Welfare ?
    We want to provide only the authentic and reliable data and do not want to leave even a minute scope for misinformation. Moreover, with intuitive analysis on this data we belive we can provide a clearer picture on the progress of COVID19.
  • How can I contribute ?
    If you want to contribute with coding, information or anything else, we will be glad to listen from you at elcomrades19@gmail.com or feel free to explore our code on Github.
  • How can I get in touch ?
    Follow us about us on Facebook and Twitter. Or drop us a few lines at elcomrades19@gmail.com.
  • I like the work, how can I help ?
    Thank you very much. We will be glad to listen from you. You can always leave us a comment on Facebook and/or Twitter.
